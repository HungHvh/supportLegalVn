## 📝 Decisions & Lessons
- **Hybrid over Dense**: Sparse search (FTS5/SPLADE) is critical for legal terms (Law numbers, Article references).
- **Hierarchical is King**: Context fragmentation in fixed-size chunking significantly degraded performance; hierarchical chunking fixed this.
- **Docker-First Execution**: Mandatory for Windows hosts to bypass native ML library DLL issues.
